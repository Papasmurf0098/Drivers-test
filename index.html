Reef Rider Adventure Polished

A one-file driving game with built-in image assets, strong roadside density, tuned pacing, minimap, music, save data, and a hub flow.

Sector 1 — Shallow Bloom

Bright lagoon roads, easy curves, and a gentle first gate.

Sector 2 — Amber Shelf

Warmer colors, tighter rhythm, and supply gathering under pressure.

Sector 3 — Mint Squall

Traffic gets meaner, scenery thickens, and boosts matter more.

Sector 4 — Sapphire Crown

Fast finale, glowing reef roads, and the clean closing sprint.

Controls: touch buttons or arrow keys / WASD. Pause with the button or P. Assets are generated inline as SVG images, so this stays portable instead of becoming dependency soup.
